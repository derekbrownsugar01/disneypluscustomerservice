About Disney+ Plus

Disney+ is a streaming service offering access to a wide range of movies, TV shows, and exclusive content from Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic. Enjoy unlimited access to your favorite titles anytime, anywhere.

Disney+ Plus Pricing

Here are the pricing options available in the United States for Disney+ Plus:

  • Basic Plan: $7.99 per month (Ad-supported)
  • Premium Plan: $11.99 per month (Ad-free)
  • Bundle Plan (Disney+, Hulu, ESPN+): Starting at $19.99 per month

If you encounter issues with your subscription or content, here are a few troubleshooting methods:

  • Ensure a stable internet connection.
  • Try logging out and logging back into your account.
  • Clear your cache and cookies in your browser.
  • Make sure your Disney+ app is up-to-date.
